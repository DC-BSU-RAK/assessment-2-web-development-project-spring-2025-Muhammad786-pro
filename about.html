About BRABUS

Founded in 1977, BRABUS has become synonymous with powerful, high-performance, and luxurious automobiles. We specialize in tuning and customizing Mercedes-Benz, Maybach, and Smart vehicles to create exceptional driving experiences.

Our team of engineers, designers, and technicians work passionately to deliver unmatched craftsmanship in every project we take on.

With over four decades of German innovation, BRABUS redefines vehicles with powerful engines, aerodynamic design, and bespoke interiors that captivate the senses. Each car is a handcrafted masterpiece.
